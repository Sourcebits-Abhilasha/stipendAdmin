Privacy Policy

The CollegeHunch™ mobile application (“the App”) is an app that will enable you, through your iOS device (“Mobile Device”), to view information about colleges and universities (“Colleges”). Among other things, it enables you to save information about particular Colleges in “Favorites” and “Clippings,” compare Colleges to one another, and add dates to your mobile calendar.

The App is provided by Stipend, LLC (“Stipend” or “we”). This policy describes the personally identifying information that we collect from you and other information that is collected passively when you use the App, what we do with it, and with whom we share it.

    Collection of information
  • Personally identifying information you provide to us directly. If you sign up for an account on the App, we ask you for your first and last name and email address, your gender, whether you are a student or a parent, and if you are a student, what high-school class you are in.
  • Other information you provide to us indirectly. The searches you conduct, the Colleges you list in your “Favorites,” the Colleges you compare, the information you save in your “Clippings,” and the way in which you filter the Colleges that you are reviewing will indicate the kind of College you may be looking for, what aspects of Colleges may be important to you, and/or in what geographical location you may want to attend College.
  • Information we may receive from third parties. We may receive personally identifying information and other information about you from third parties. For example, if you access the App through a third-party connection or log-in, such as Twitter® or Facebook® Connect, or by “following” or “liking” the App, or linking the App to that service, that third party may pass certain information about your use of its service to us. Such information may include the user ID associated with your account (such as your Facebook I.D.), an access token necessary to access that service, any information that you have permitted the third party to share with us, and any information you have made public in connection with that service. You should always review, and if necessary, adjust your privacy settings on third-party websites and services before linking or connecting them to the App.
  • Device identifiers. When you access the App through your Mobile Device, we may access, collect, monitor and/or remotely store information about your Mobile Device, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifier browser type and language, serial number, mobile device phone number, and mobile network information.
  • Location data.  When you access the App through your Mobile Device, we may access, collect, monitor and/or remotely store “Location Data,” which may include GPS coordinates (e.g. latitude and/or longitude) or similar information regarding the location of your Mobile Device. Location Data may convey to us information about how you browse and use the App.  
    Use of information
  • To help you find Colleges. We may use your Device Identifier to provide you with information about Colleges that are similar to or located near a College you have looked at or are looking at, information about Colleges and points of interest in the geographical area in or from which you are using the App, and other customized information that we think may be useful to you in your search for a College.
  • To facilitate use of the CollegeHunch™ App. We may use cookies, log files, Device Identifiers, Location Data and other technologies to (i) remember you so that you will not have to log in each time you use the App, (ii) to monitor your individual click behavior (how you browse and use the App), (iii) to monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of users, traffic, and demographic patterns, (iv) diagnose or fix technology problems, (v) help you efficiently access your information after you sign in, and (vi) automatically update the App on your Mobile Device.  
  • To send you service and promotional information. We may use your email address, and other information we collect or receive through the App to send you reminders, changes/updates, and technical and security notices related to the App, and promotions and special offers related to the App or other products and services, by emails or push notifications to your Mobile Device.

If you are a California resident, and you want to find out the names of any third parties with whom we share your personally identifying information under California’s privacy law (Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 22575 et seq.), send a request to info@collegehunch.com and within thirty (30) days, we will provide you with that information.
